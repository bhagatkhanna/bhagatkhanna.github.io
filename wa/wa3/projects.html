Micro-camper Van Reconversion

This fall I am reconverting my Kia Sedona into a micro-camper that I can use to take climbing trips.

Previously I lived full-time in the van for nine months with a folding futon/bed, sink/fridge vanity,

and enought storage for all my belongings. The reason for taking apart version one of the van was to

reinstall the back row of seats and have it be functional as a car for more then just two poeple.

This reconversion will tackle how the van can be maximally usable as both a car and a camper, while

also providing a chance to up the craftsmanship.
